Portfolio Modeling

Analyzed beta correlations of individual stocks compared to the overall market under bullish/bearish conditions. These "biased" beta values represent strong movement with up markets and weaker correlations with down markets.

Took advantage of favorable beta correlations to create portfolios that mirror that of the market, removing the least effective stocks to improve overall performance (called the Dow 29).

Compared portfolio performance over time using Monte Carlo simulations to mitigate effects from return erosion.

Tetris Artificial Intelligence System

This Tetris simulation includes an AI that can clear thousands of lines in seconds. It is implemented using a greedy algorithm that assigns all possible board states to a score before choosing the move that yields the highest-scoring state.

This score is weighted on a combination of covariates, including the maximum height, number of holes, and jaggedness.
